Have you heard of the incredible rainbow of color variation in strawberry poison frogs (O. pumilio) in Bocas del Toro, Panama? A new study addressing this perplexing system, lead by recent Nielsen lab alum Dr. Diana Aguilar-Gómez, was just published in Current Biology.
